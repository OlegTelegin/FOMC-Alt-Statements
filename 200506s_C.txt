>> Alternative C

4. Pressures on inflation have picked up further in recent months, although measures of longer-term inflation expectations remain well contained.
5. The Committee will respond to changes in economic prospects as needed to fulfill its obligation to foster the attainment of both sustainable economic growth and maintain price stability.
